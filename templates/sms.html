About Our Model

Inbox Guard utilizes advanced machine learning techniques including Naive Bayes and Logistic Regression algorithms to achieve superior spam detection accuracy. Our model has been trained on extensive real-world SMS datasets, incorporating natural language processing and feature engineering to understand message patterns, sender behavior, and content characteristics.


The system continuously learns from new data patterns while maintaining privacy and security standards. With over 95% accuracy rate, Inbox Guard provides reliable protection against evolving spam and phishing techniques.
