Why Do We Need a Spam Classifier?

Every day, millions of users receive SMS containing promotions, phishing links, and scams. These not only waste time but can lead to financial and personal losses.

A spam SMS classifier helps automatically detect and filter out such messages, ensuring only safe and relevant messages reach your attention.
